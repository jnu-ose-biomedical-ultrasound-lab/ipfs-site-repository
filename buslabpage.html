Welcome to the Biomedical Ultrasound Lab at Jeju National University's Ocean Systems Engineering. Our current work involves simulation of blood circulation, ultrasound neuroimaging, bio-acoustics, non-invasive neuromodulation, and improvement of Kranion software.


Here is a list of our published work. Our recent work includes:


Depletion-model-based numerical simulation of the kinetics of red blood cell aggregation under sinusoidal pulsatile flow

Cheong-Ah Lee, Qi Kong, Dong-Guk Paeng


Asymmetric pulsation of rat carotid artery bifurcation in three-dimension observed by ultrasound imaging

Changzhu Jin, Kweon-Ho Nam, Dong-Guk Paeng
